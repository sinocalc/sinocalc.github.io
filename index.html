Damage Calculator
  1. Fill in the P.Atk and M.Atk values.
  2. Select the weapon type for each slot. If slot is empty, leave it on none.
  3. Select the skill tier for each weapon and fill in the skill level.
  4. If the weapon has Dauntless Courage, select the tier and fill in the skill level.
  5. Grid and weapon names are optional, use them at your convinience.
  6. Click the calculate button once completed.
Grid Comparator
  • Click compare grids to open second grid input.
  • Fill out, copy, or import a second grid.
  • Click the compare button once both grids are completed.
Analysis
  • The calculator determines average weapon damage and total damage of one rotation.
  • DC average is calculated with proc rate and skill level.
  • AoE average is calculated with range of targets.
  • The comparator will find the grid with greater total damage from one rotation.
  • The damage differential may vary depending on enemy defense and combo.
  • Try different defense and combo values to check damage output and comparison.
  • Use the information to refine your grid and determine the best setup.
